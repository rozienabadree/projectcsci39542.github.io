Abstract

Uber has recently driven the once-solid yellow cab industry into bankruptcy. I intend on taking a deep-dive into the available data to analyze and visualize Uber’s rapid increase in rides, the yellow cab’s rapid decrease in rides, and compare Uber's and the yellow taxi’s fares/fees (not part of the data analysis but important to discuss).
